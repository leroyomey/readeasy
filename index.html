Privacy Policy for ReadEasy

Effective Date: [9/13/2024]

At ReadEasy, we prioritize your privacy and security. This privacy policy explains what data we collect when you use the ReadEasy Chrome extension, how we use it, and your rights regarding your information.

1. Information We Collect

ReadEasy does not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (PII). The extension operates locally on your device and only interacts with the web pages you visit to adjust font styles.

2. How We Use Your Information

We store your font preferences locally on your device to:

  • Apply your selected font on the active tab.
No data is transmitted or shared with external servers or third parties.

3. Third-Party Links

ReadEasy does not interact with or include third-party services, links, or external APIs. The extension strictly modifies the font styling on the pages you visit, without collecting or transmitting any data.

4. Your Rights

Since ReadEasy does not collect or store personal information, there are no personal data rights to manage. You can uninstall the extension at any time, and all font preferences stored locally will be removed.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the effective date at the top of this policy. We encourage you to periodically review this privacy policy to stay informed about how we protect your data.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this privacy policy or the ReadEasy extension, please contact us at: therealreadeasy@gmail.com.
